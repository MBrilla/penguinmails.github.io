# Client Onboarding Workflow

Effective client onboarding establishes the foundation for successful engagements. This document provides discovery frameworks, assessment templates, and proposal formats for professional client management.

## Discovery and Assessment Framework

### Client Assessment Template

#### Business Overview

- Industry and market position
- Current email marketing status
- Goals and objectives
- Target audience and customer journey
- Existing marketing technology stack

#### Email Marketing Assessment

- Current subscriber list size and quality
- Existing email templates and design
- Current automation and segmentation
- Performance benchmarks and metrics
- Competitor analysis and positioning

#### Strategic Planning Session

- Goal setting and success metrics definition
- Budget and timeline establishment
- Resource allocation and priority setting
- Timeline and milestone development
- Risk assessment and mitigation planning

## Project Initiation Process

### Phase 1: Setup and Configuration (Week 1)

- Account and branding setup
- Template development and approval
- Initial audience segmentation
- Automation framework development

### Phase 2: Content Development (Week 2)

- Campaign content creation
- Automated sequence development
- Quality assurance and testing
- Performance tracking setup

### Phase 3: Launch and Optimization (Week 3)

- Campaign launch and monitoring
- Initial performance analysis
- Optimization recommendations
- Client training and handoff

## Proposal and Presentation Formats

### Professional Proposal Template

#### Executive Summary

- Client situation and challenges
- Proposed solution and approach
- Expected outcomes and ROI
- Investment and timeline summary

#### Detailed Service Description

- Service package overview
- Specific deliverables and milestones
- Process and methodology explanation
- Quality assurance and review procedures

#### Success Metrics and KPIs

- Primary performance indicators
- Secondary success measures
- Reporting and communication schedule
- Optimization and improvement processes

#### Investment and Terms

- Service package pricing
- Payment terms and schedule
- Additional services and options
- Terms and conditions summary

## Discovery Question Set

### Business and Goals

1. What are your primary business objectives for email marketing?
2. Who is your target audience and customer personas?
3. What is your current email marketing maturity level?
4. What challenges are you facing with your current email marketing?
5. What timeline and budget do you have for email marketing initiatives?

### Technical and Resources

1. What email marketing platform are you currently using?
2. What is your current subscriber list size and quality?
3. What content creation resources do you have available?
4. What integration needs do you have with other marketing tools?
5. What reporting and analytics requirements do you have?

### Strategy and Expectations

1. What success metrics and KPIs are most important to you?
2. What is your preferred communication style and frequency?
3. What are your expectations for timeline and deliverables?
4. What additional services or support do you anticipate needing?
5. What concerns or constraints should we be aware of?
