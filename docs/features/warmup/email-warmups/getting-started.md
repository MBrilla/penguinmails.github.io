# Getting Started with Email Warm-up

Complete your email warm-up setup in 5 minutes with our automated configuration system.

## Level 1: Instant Setup (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create Email Account

One-click account provisioning with automatic domain setup. Our system handles the technical configuration so you can focus on your campaigns.

### Step 2: Automated Verification

DNS, SPF, DKIM, and DMARC configuration happens automatically. You'll receive confirmation once verification completes.

### Step 3: Smart Configuration

Optimal warm-up settings are applied based on your target audience and industry. The system analyzes your use case and configures accordingly.

### Step 4: Immediate Start

Warm-up sequence begins instantly upon setup completion. You'll see progress in your dashboard within hours.

## Level 2: Professional Setup

### Account Setup

1. Create your free email account through our platform
2. Verify the account through our automated process
3. Configure basic settings and preferences

### Configure Warm-up Settings

- **Sending Schedule**: Choose optimal send times for your target audience
- **Email Templates**: Use our proven human-like warm-up templates
- **Target List**: Upload contacts for warm-up sequence
- **Progress Monitoring**: Set up alerts and notifications

### Start Warm-up

- Begin with our automated 21-day warm-up sequence
- Monitor progress through your dashboard
- Make adjustments based on real-time feedback
- Gradually transition to full cold email campaigns

## Prerequisites

Before starting warm-up, ensure you have:

- **Domain Access**: Access to DNS management for your domain
- **Email Provider Account**: Email account ready for warm-up
- **Target Audience**: Defined warm-up recipient list
- **Compliance Setup**: Configured regulatory compliance settings
