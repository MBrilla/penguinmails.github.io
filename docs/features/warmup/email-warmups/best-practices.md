# Email Warm-up Best Practices

Follow these guidelines to maximize your warm-up success and avoid common pitfalls.

## Essential Guidelines

### Email Content

- Use conversational, personal emails
- Avoid obvious sales language initially
- Include relevant, valuable content
- Keep messages short and engaging
- Use proper grammar and spelling

### Timing Optimization

- Send emails during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
- Avoid weekends for business outreach
- Consider your target audience's time zone
- Space emails appropriately throughout the day
- Test different send times to find optimal windows

### List Quality

- Use verified, high-quality contact lists
- Remove bounced or invalid email addresses
- Segment lists by engagement level
- Avoid purchasing low-quality email lists
- Regularly clean and update your contacts

## Success Metrics

### Key Performance Indicators

| Metric | Target | Action if Below |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| **Sender Score** | 70+ | Reduce volume, improve content |
| **Inbox Placement** | 90%+ | Extend warm-up period |
| **Bounce Rate** | <2% | Clean email lists |
| **Spam Complaint Rate** | <0.1% | Review targeting and content |

### Monitoring Framework

- **Daily Checks**: Basic reputation and deliverability monitoring
- **Weekly Analysis**: Performance trend analysis and optimization
- **Monthly Reviews**: Comprehensive strategy assessment and planning

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Critical Mistakes

These errors destroy deliverability:

- **Sending Too Fast**: Starting with high volumes (100+ emails) immediately
- **Poor Content Quality**: Sales-focused content during warm-up
- **Inconsistent Patterns**: Irregular sending patterns flag accounts

Learn more in our [deliverability myths guide](/guides/deliverability-myths-debunked).

### Timing Errors

- **Ignoring Time Zones**: Not considering prospect locations
- **Weekend Sending**: Sending business emails on weekends
- **Holiday Overlap**: Not accounting for business closures
- **Frequency Mistakes**: Sending too frequently or irregularly

### Content Mistakes

- **Generic Templates**: Using one-size-fits-all approaches
- **Poor Personalization**: Insufficient or incorrect personalization
- **Link-Heavy Content**: Too many links in warm-up emails
- **Attachment Issues**: Sending attachments before reputation established

### Technical Mistakes

- **DNS Issues**: Improper SPF, DKIM, or DMARC configuration
- **Domain Problems**: Using suspicious or low-quality domains
- **IP Reputation**: Not monitoring IP reputation across providers
- **List Quality**: Using purchased or outdated contact lists

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

| Issue | Immediate Action | Solution |
|-------|------------------|----------|
| **Low Sender Score** | Reduce volume temporarily | Extend warm-up with improved content |
| **High Bounce Rates** | Pause sending immediately | Clean lists and restart |
| **Spam Complaints** | Stop sending | Review content quality and targeting |

### Performance Optimization

- **Slow Progress**: Adjust progression timeline based on performance
- **Inconsistent Results**: Standardize processes and monitor closely
- **Volume Limitations**: Optimize sending patterns for better capacity
