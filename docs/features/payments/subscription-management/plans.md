# Subscription Plans

PenguinMails offers four subscription tiers to match your business needs, plus flexible add-ons.

## Starter Plan

**Perfect for small teams getting started**

### Pricing

$49/month or $470/year (save $118)

### Includes

- 10,000 emails/month
- 2 team members
- 3 workspaces
- 5 custom domains
- Basic analytics
- Email support

### Best For

Freelancers, small agencies, startups

---

## Professional Plan

**Most Popular - For growing businesses**

### Pricing

$149/month or $1,430/year (save $358)

### Includes

- 50,000 emails/month
- 5 team members
- 10 workspaces
- 15 custom domains
- Enhanced analytics
- Priority email support
- CRM integrations
- API access

### Best For

Growing agencies, marketing teams, SMBs

---

## Business Plan

**For established businesses with high volume**

### Pricing

$399/month or $3,830/year (save $958)

### Includes

- 200,000 emails/month
- 15 team members
- Unlimited workspaces
- Unlimited custom domains
- Advanced analytics
- Email + chat support
- All integrations
- Dedicated IP (1 included)
- Advanced automation

### Best For

Established agencies, enterprise marketing teams

---

## Enterprise Plan

**Custom solutions for large organizations**

### Pricing

Custom (Contact Sales)

### Includes

- Unlimited emails
- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited workspaces
- Unlimited custom domains
- Real-time analytics
- 24/7 phone + dedicated support
- SSO (SAML, OAuth)
- Advanced security features
- Dedicated IPs (multiple)
- Custom SLA
- Onboarding & training
- Dedicated account manager

### Best For

Large enterprises, white-label partners

## Usage-Based Add-Ons

**Need more than your plan includes?**

| Add-On | Price | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Extra Emails** | $10/10k emails | Additional email sends beyond plan limit |
| **Additional Users** | $15/user/month | Extra team member seats |
| **Dedicated IP** | $50/IP/month | Dedicated sending IP address |
| **Extra Workspaces** | $20/workspace/month | Additional workspace (Starter/Pro only) |
| **Priority Support** | $99/month | 24/7 support upgrade (Starter/Pro) |
