# Domain Management

## Basic Domain Selection

- **Professional Domains**: Choose business-appropriate domains
- **Brand Alignment**: Match domains to your company name

## Strategic Management

### Domain Selection Strategy

- **Industry Relevance**: Choose domains that match your business sector
- **Memorability**: Select easy-to-remember domain names
- **Professional Appearance**: Avoid suspicious or spammy-sounding domains
- **Geographic Considerations**: Consider target market locations

### DNS Configuration

- **SPF Records**: Automatically configure SPF for email authentication
- **DKIM Setup**: DomainKeys Identified Mail for email integrity
- **DMARC Policy**: Domain-based Message Authentication policies
- **MX Records**: Mail exchanger records for proper email routing

### SSL Certificate Management

- **Automatic SSL**: Free SSL certificates for secure email transmission
- **Certificate Renewal**: Automatic renewal of SSL certificates
- **Security Monitoring**: Continuous SSL certificate health monitoring
- **Compliance**: Ensure compliance with email security standards
