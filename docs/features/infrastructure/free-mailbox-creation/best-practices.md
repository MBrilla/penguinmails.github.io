# Best Practices for Email Accounts

## Essential Guidelines

- **Professional Format**: Use business-appropriate naming conventions
- **Consistent Branding**: Maintain consistent email signatures and branding
- **Security Basics**: Use strong passwords and basic security practices

## Professional Practices

### Account Naming Conventions

- **Professional Format**: Use business-appropriate naming conventions
- **Role-Based Accounts**: Create accounts based on business roles (sales@, support@)
- **Name-Based Accounts**: Personal accounts using team member names
- **Department Accounts**: Functional accounts by department or service

### Profile Optimization

- **Professional Signatures**: Create consistent, professional email signatures
- **Profile Photos**: Use professional headshots for personal accounts
- **Bio Information**: Complete professional profiles and bio information
- **Contact Information**: Include relevant business contact details

### Reputation Building

- **Gradual Warm-up**: Start with low sending volumes
- **Content Quality**: Send only high-quality, relevant content
- **Professional Communication**: Maintain professional tone in all interactions
- **Compliance**: Ensure all communications meet legal requirements
