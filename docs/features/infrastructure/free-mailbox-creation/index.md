## Why Create Multiple Email Accounts

### Basic Benefits

- **Professional Appearance**: Appear more legitimate and trustworthy to prospects
- **Better Organization**: Separate business communications from personal emails
- **Deliverability Protection**: Distribute risk across multiple accounts

### Strategic Benefits

- **Deliverability Optimization**: Multiple email addresses enable better sender reputation management, risk distribution, testing different approaches, and maintaining deliverability if one account gets flagged
- **Campaign Scaling**: Additional email addresses allow scaling campaigns, testing messaging, targeting different segments, and maintaining organization
