# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Comprehensive KPI framework with actionable insights for strategic decision-making and performance optimization.

## Essential Business Metrics

Critical metrics to track daily for business health:

| Metric | Description | Target |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Inbox Placement Rate | Emails landing in inbox vs spam | >95% |
| Response Rate | Percentage who replied | >2% |
| Bounce Rate | Percentage that bounced | <2% |
| MRR | Monthly Recurring Revenue | $50K by month 12 |
| CAC | Customer Acquisition Cost | <$200 |
| LTV | Customer Lifetime Value | >$2,000 |

## North Star & Core Business Metrics

- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)** - Primary business health indicator
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)** - Target: >$2,000 per customer
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** - Target: <$200 per customer
- **Net Revenue Retention** - Target: >110% (expansion revenue focus)
- **Monthly Churn Rate** - Target: <5% for sustainable growth

## Detailed Performance Metrics

### Engagement Metrics

- **Open Rate**: Percentage of recipients who opened your emails
- **Click-Through Rate**: Percentage who clicked links in your emails
- **Meeting Booking Rate**: Percentage who booked a meeting or call

### Conversion Metrics

- **Lead Quality Score**: Assessment of prospect quality based on responses
- **Pipeline Conversion**: How many responses convert to sales opportunities
- **Revenue Attribution**: Revenue generated from cold email campaigns
- **Customer Acquisition Cost**: Cost per customer acquired through cold email

### Operational Metrics

- **Account Performance**: Performance breakdown by email account
- **Template Effectiveness**: Which email templates perform best
- **Timing Optimization**: Best send times for your audience
- **List Quality Analysis**: Performance by contact list segment

## Strategic Business Analytics

### Advanced Performance Indicators

- **Predictive Scoring**: AI-powered prospect scoring and prioritization
- **Segmentation Analysis**: Performance by industry, company size, and role
- **ROI Tracking**: Complete return on investment calculation
- **Lifetime Value Analysis**: Long-term value tracking for acquired customers

### System Performance KPIs

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Uptime | 99.9% availability |

### Enterprise Reporting

- **Compliance Metrics**: GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and regulatory tracking

## Detailed KPI Formulas & Targets

### Financial & Growth KPIs

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
- Formula: Total marketing + sales spend / Number of new customers
- Target: <$200 per customer, rolling 12-month average

**Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**
- Formula: Average revenue per user Ã— customer lifespan
- Target: >$2,000 per customer with 3:1 LTV/CAC ratio

**Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**
- Formula: Sum of all active subscriptions
- Target: $50K by month 12, $200K by month 24

**Net Revenue Retention**
- Formula: (Starting ARR + Expansion - Churn) / Starting ARR
- Target: >110% through expansion revenue focus

### Email Performance KPIs

**Deliverability Rate**
- Formula: Emails delivered / Emails sent
- Target: >98% for premium performance

**Inbox Placement Rate**
- Formula: Emails in inbox / Emails delivered
- Target: >95% across all major email providers

**Bounce Rate**
- Formula: Hard bounces / Total sends
- Target: <2% with automated alerts at 3%

**Sender Reputation Score**
- Formula: Composite score from multiple reputation providers
- Target: >85/100 across Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook

### Product & Engagement KPIs

**Time to First Value**
- Formula: Time from signup to first successful email send
- Target: <30 minutes for optimal user experience

**Feature Adoption Rate**
- Formula: Users using feature / Total eligible users
- Target: >60% for core features, >40% for advanced features

**Customer Satisfaction (NPS)**
- Formula: % Promoters - % Detractors
- Target: >50 with >70% response rate

### Operational Excellence KPIs

**Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR)**
- Target: <4 hours for critical, <24 hours for normal

**Deployment Frequency**
- Target: Weekly production deployments with <5% failure rate

**Change Failure Rate**
- Target: <5% deployment failure rate with automated rollback
