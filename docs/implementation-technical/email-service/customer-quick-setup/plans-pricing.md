# Cold Email Service Plans

Our pricing is designed around cold email sending volume and feature access rather than complex infrastructure plans.

## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Starter | Growth | Pro |
|---------|---------|--------|-----|
| **Price** | $29/month | $79/month | $199/month |
| **Cold Emails** | 2,000/month | 10,000/month | 50,000/month |
| **Email Accounts** | 3 | 10 | Unlimited |
| **Warm-up** | Basic | AI-optimized | AI + smart algorithms |
| **Analytics** | Simple | Advanced + A/B | Comprehensive |
| **Support** | Email | Priority email + chat | Dedicated manager |

## Starter Plan - $29/month

**Target**: Individual sales reps, small businesses

### Included Features

- Up to 2,000 cold emails per month
- 3 email accounts included
- Basic warm-up automation
- Simple analytics dashboard
- Unified inbox for responses
- Email support

**Ideal For**: Getting started with cold email outreach

## Growth Plan - $79/month

**Target**: Sales teams, growing businesses

### Included Features

- Up to 10,000 cold emails per month
- 10 email accounts included
- Advanced warm-up with AI optimization
- Inbox rotation to avoid detection
- Advanced analytics and A/B testing
- Response tracking and follow-up automation
- Priority email and chat support

**Ideal For**: Scaling cold email campaigns

## Pro Plan - $199/month

**Target**: Agencies, sales teams, marketing teams

### Included Features

- Up to 50,000 cold emails per month
- Unlimited email accounts
- White-label platform options
- Advanced inbox rotation with smart algorithms
- Comprehensive analytics and reporting
- CRM integrations
- Team collaboration tools
- Dedicated success manager
- Priority support

**Ideal For**: Professional cold email operations

## Add-on Features

Enhance any plan with additional capabilities:

| Add-on | Price | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Additional Email Accounts | $5/account/month | Scale beyond plan limits |
| Extra Cold Email Volume | $0.02/email | Additional sending capacity |
| Advanced Analytics | $29/month | Detailed reporting and insights |
| CRM Integrations | $49/month | Connect to Salesforce, HubSpot, etc. |

## Plan Selection Guide

### Choose Starter If

- You're new to cold email outreach
- Sending fewer than 2,000 emails monthly
- Operating as an individual or small team
- Budget-conscious with basic needs

### Choose Growth If

- Scaling your outreach efforts
- Need AI-powered optimization
- Running A/B tests regularly
- Managing multiple campaigns simultaneously

### Choose Pro If

- Operating an agency with multiple clients
- Need white-label capabilities
- Require comprehensive analytics
- Want dedicated support and guidance

## Billing Information

- **Billing Cycle**: Monthly or annual (annual saves 20%)
- **Payment Methods**: Credit card, bank transfer (enterprise)
- **Upgrades**: Instant, prorated billing
- **Downgrades**: Effective at next billing cycle
- **Cancellation**: Cancel anytime, access until period end
