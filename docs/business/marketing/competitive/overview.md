# Marketing Competitive Intelligence Framework

Competitive intelligence framework with clear separation between MVP scope (2025) and Post-MVP roadmap (2026+) capabilities.

**MVP Focus:** Manual analysis, basic monitoring, quarterly reporting
**Post-MVP Focus:** AI-powered intelligence, real-time monitoring, predictive analytics

## Document Structure

This framework is organized into focused modules:

1. **[Intelligence Foundation](/docs/business/marketing/competitive/foundation)** - Vision, governance, and market intelligence
2. **[Competitive Analysis](/docs/business/marketing/competitive/analysis)** - Competitor analysis and positioning
3. **[Strategic Advantage](/docs/business/marketing/competitive/advantage)** - Competitive advantage and opportunity identification
4. **[Implementation](/docs/business/marketing/competitive/implementation)** - Technology, metrics, and roadmap

## MVP Vision and Objectives

**MVP Vision:** Achieve basic marketing competitive positioning through manual intelligence gathering, basic analysis, and directional strategic positioning

### MVP Core Objectives

| Objective | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Intelligence Foundation | Basic competitive intelligence for directional advantage |
| Market Positioning | Basic market positioning through manual analysis |
| Strategic Foundation | Foundation for future competitive intelligence |
| Opportunity Identification | Basic identification of opportunities and threats |

### MVP Competitive Benefits

| Benefit | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Strategic Foundation | Basic competitive positioning |
| Market Foundation | Basic market positioning capabilities |
| Opportunity Value | $150K annual value |
| Risk Mitigation | Weekly risk identification |
