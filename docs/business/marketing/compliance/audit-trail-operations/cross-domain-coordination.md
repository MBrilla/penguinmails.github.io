# Cross-Domain Audit Coordination

Cross-domain audit coordination ensures unified tracking, consistent reporting, and seamless integration across all business domains.

## MVP Inter-Domain Audit Integration

### Unified Audit Framework

- **Audit Event Correlation:** Cross-domain event linking, relationship mapping, impact analysis
- **Unified Access Tracking:** Single sign-on integration, access pattern analysis, privilege validation
- **Data Flow Auditing:** End-to-end data flow tracking, privacy compliance, quality validation
- **Integration Health Monitoring:** Cross-domain integration health, performance tracking, error management
- **Compliance Synchronization:** Regulatory compliance coordination, unified reporting, audit preparation

### Business Domain Audit Coordination

#### Sales Domain Audit Integration

| Integration Area | Coverage |
|-----------------|----------|
| Lead Data Audit Trail | Lead generation to conversion tracking with privacy validation |
| CRM Integration Auditing | Data synchronization validation with compliance verification |
| Customer Journey Integration | Marketing to sales handoff with accountability tracking |

#### Product Domain Audit Integration

| Integration Area | Coverage |
|-----------------|----------|
| Feature Adoption Analytics | Product marketing impact measurement with validation |
| User Feedback Integration | Feedback collection to product improvement tracking |
| Development Insights | Marketing analytics to product development attribution |

#### Customer Success Domain Audit Integration

| Integration Area | Coverage |
|-----------------|----------|

| Retention Analytics Integration | Marketing attribution to retention with validation |

#### Finance Domain Audit Integration

| Integration Area | Coverage |

## MVP Audit Trail Analytics and Reporting

### Basic Audit Analytics

- **Basic Ingestion:** Weekly audit event collection with validation and enrichment
- **Pattern Recognition:** Basic anomaly detection, trend analysis, risk identification
- **Compliance Monitoring:** Weekly compliance checking with manual alerting
- **Performance Analytics:** Audit trail effectiveness measurement with optimization
- **Basic Analytics:** Compliance risk analysis with proactive mitigation

## MVP Automated Audit Procedures

### Continuous Audit Validation

- **Audit Trail Completeness:** Weekly validation of audit log completeness and accuracy
- **Compliance Validation:** Weekly checking of compliance requirements with violation detection
- **Integration Validation:** Weekly testing of cross-domain audit trail integration
- **Quality Assurance:** Weekly quality checks on audit data with correction procedures
- **Performance Monitoring:** Audit system performance tracking with optimization

### Audit Automation Framework

- **Event Collection Automation:** Automated collection of audit events across all systems
- **Compliance Checking Automation:** Weekly compliance validation with violation alerting
- **Reporting Automation:** Weekly compliance and performance reporting with distribution
- **Alert System Automation:** Weekly alerting for compliance violations and system issues

## MVP Business Continuity and Audit Preservation

### Audit Data Protection and Retention

#### Audit Data Security

- **Audit Log Encryption:** End-to-end encryption with key management
- **Access Control:** Role-based access with approval workflows
- **Data Integrity:** Hash-based validation with tamper detection
- **Backup and Recovery:** Secure backup with disaster recovery procedures
- **Long-Term Preservation:** Archival with format migration and accessibility

#### Retention and Archival

- **Regulatory Retention:** Compliance with legal requirements
- **Business Retention:** Business-driven retention policies
- **Automated Retention:** Automated deletion and archival based on policies
- **Archive Access:** Secure access to historical audit data
- **Format Migration:** Automated migration to current formats
