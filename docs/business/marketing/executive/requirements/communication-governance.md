# Communication Matrix & Governance

Executive stakeholder communication requirements and governance framework.

## Executive Stakeholder Communication Matrix

### CMO Communication Requirements

#### Strategic Reporting Framework

**Primary Objective**: Establish CMO communication framework for executive visibility and decision-making

**Communication Channels**:
- Executive Dashboard: Weekly access to marketing performance and ROI metrics
- Monthly Reports: Comprehensive monthly marketing performance and strategic reports
- Quarterly Reviews: Quarterly strategic review and optimization sessions
- Annual Planning: Annual strategic planning and marketing roadmap development

**Key Performance Indicators**:
- Marketing ROI: Weekly tracking of marketing return on investment
- Revenue Impact: Marketing contribution to overall business revenue and growth
- Market Position: Marketing impact on competitive positioning and market share
- Strategic Alignment: Marketing alignment with business strategy and objectives

### Marketing Director Communication Requirements

#### Operational Reporting Framework

**Primary Objective**: Establish Marketing Director communication framework for team management and optimization

**Communication Channels**:
- Daily Briefings: Daily marketing performance briefings and optimization updates
- Weekly Reports: Weekly marketing performance reports and team activity updates
- Monthly Reviews: Monthly team performance reviews and strategic alignment sessions
- Quarterly Planning: Quarterly marketing planning and strategic preparation

**Key Performance Indicators**:
- Campaign Performance: Real-time campaign performance metrics and optimization
- Team Productivity: Marketing team productivity and efficiency metrics
- Budget Utilization: Marketing budget utilization and optimization metrics
- Strategic Execution: Marketing strategic initiative execution and progress

### Cross-Functional Communication Requirements

#### Marketing-Sales Alignment

- Lead Handoff: Marketing-Sales lead handoff optimization and tracking
- Performance Feedback: Sales feedback on marketing lead quality and conversion
- Joint Planning: Marketing-Sales joint planning and strategy development
- Shared Metrics: Shared marketing and sales performance metrics and KPIs

#### Marketing-Product Alignment

- Product Launches: Marketing coordination with product launches and releases
- Message Optimization: Product feedback on marketing messaging and positioning
- Feature Promotion: Marketing promotion of product features and capabilities
- Market Feedback: Product feedback on market needs and customer requirements

## Executive Governance Framework

### CMO Governance Structure

#### Strategic Decision-Making Framework

- Strategic Planning: CMO leadership of annual marketing strategic planning and roadmap
- Investment Decisions: CMO authority over marketing technology investments and budget allocation
- Performance Oversight: CMO oversight of marketing performance and optimization
- Vendor Management: CMO management of strategic marketing vendor relationships and partnerships

#### Marketing Technology Governance

- Technology Strategy: CMO input on marketing technology strategy and roadmap
- Platform Selection: CMO approval of marketing technology platform selections and integrations
- Performance Monitoring: CMO monitoring of marketing technology performance and optimization
- Innovation Leadership: CMO leadership of marketing technology innovation and advancement

## Implementation Success Framework

### CMO Implementation Leadership

- Strategic Oversight: CMO strategic oversight of implementation planning and execution
- Budget Authority: CMO authority over implementation budget and resource allocation
- Stakeholder Management: CMO management of executive stakeholder relationships and communication
- Success Measurement: CMO measurement of implementation success and business value delivery

### Marketing Director Implementation Support

- Operational Planning: Marketing Director operational planning for implementation activities
- Team Coordination: Marketing Director coordination of marketing team implementation support
- Performance Monitoring: Marketing Director monitoring of implementation impact on team performance
- Training and Development: Marketing Director coordination of team training and development for new systems

## Executive Success Metrics

### CMO Success Metrics (MVP)

- Marketing ROI: >150% return on basic marketing technology investment
- Revenue Impact: Basic marketing contribution to business revenue growth and targets
- Market Position: Basic improvement in competitive positioning and market share
- Strategic Alignment: 85% alignment between basic marketing strategy and business objectives

### Marketing Director Success Metrics (MVP)

- Campaign Performance: 15% improvement in basic campaign performance metrics
- Team Productivity: 25% improvement in basic marketing team productivity and efficiency
- Budget Optimization: 10% improvement in basic marketing budget utilization and ROI
- Strategic Execution: 90% completion of CMO basic strategic initiatives and priorities
