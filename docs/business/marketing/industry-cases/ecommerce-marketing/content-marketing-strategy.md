# E-commerce Content Marketing Strategy

This section covers MVP-phase product-focused content marketing and distribution strategies.

---

## Product-Focused Content

### Content Types by Customer Journey Stage

#### Discovery Content

Content designed to attract and engage potential customers:

- **Basic product photography and videos:** High-quality visual content
- **Basic comparison guides and buying guides:** Help customers make decisions
- **Basic style guides and content:** Inspirational and educational content
- **Basic user-generated content campaigns:** Leverage customer-created content

---

#### Purchase Content

Content designed to support the purchase decision:

- **Basic product specifications and features:** Detailed product information
- **Customer reviews and ratings display:** Social proof and validation
- **Basic FAQ content and checkout copy:** Remove purchase barriers

---

#### Post-Purchase Content

Content designed to enhance customer satisfaction and retention:

- **Basic tutorials and how-to content:** Help customers get value
- **Basic care and maintenance guides:** Extend product life
- **Basic styling tips and combinations:** Increase product usage
- **Customer testimonials and case studies:** Build community and trust

---

## Content Distribution Strategy

### Owned Channels

Channels you control and can optimize directly:

| Channel | Content Type | Frequency |
|---------|--------------|-----------|
| Product Detail Pages | Rich media, specs, reviews | Continuous updates |
| Blog | Educational, buying guides | Weekly |
| Email Newsletter | Curated selections, tips | Weekly/Biweekly |
| Social Media | Product showcases, lifestyle | Daily |

---

### Earned Channels

Third-party channels where you can earn visibility:

- **Basic product review sites and partnerships:** Get featured on review platforms
- **Basic blog features and collaborations:** Guest posting and features
- **Basic social media mentions and shares:** Encourage organic sharing
- **Basic customer testimonials and case studies:** Leverage customer stories

---

### Paid Channels

Channels where you invest in reach:

| Channel | Content Type | Focus |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| Google Shopping Ads | Product listings | Product optimization |
| Facebook/Instagram | Product posts | Visual engagement |
| Pinterest | Promoted pins | Discovery and inspiration |
| YouTube | Product demonstrations | Video content |

---

## Content Creation Framework

### Content Planning Process

1. **Identify Customer Needs:** What questions do customers have?
2. **Map to Journey Stage:** When do they need this information?
3. **Choose Format:** What format best delivers the content?
4. **Create and Optimize:** Produce high-quality content
5. **Distribute and Promote:** Get content to the right audience
6. **Measure and Iterate:** Track performance and improve

### Content Quality Standards

- **Visual Quality:** Professional photography and video
- **Information Accuracy:** Verified product specifications
- **SEO Optimization:** Keyword research and optimization
- **Mobile Optimization:** Content readable on all devices
- **Accessibility:** Alt text, captions, readable fonts

## Content Calendar Template

### Monthly Content Plan

| Week | Blog | Email | Social | Paid |
|------|------|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Buying guide | Newsletter | Daily posts | Shopping ads |
| 2 | How-to tutorial | Product feature | Daily posts | Retargeting |
| 3 | Customer story | Tips email | Daily posts | Social ads |
| 4 | Trend content | Monthly recap | Daily posts | Shopping ads |

### Seasonal Content Considerations

- Plan content 6-8 weeks ahead for major seasons
- Create evergreen content that can be refreshed
- Develop templates for recurring content types
- Build a content asset library for reuse

## Success Metrics

### Content Performance KPIs

- **Traffic:** Organic visits to content pages
- **Engagement:** Time on page, scroll depth, shares
- **Conversion:** Content-attributed purchases
- **SEO:** Keyword rankings, backlinks

### Content ROI Measurement

Track the contribution of content to:

- Customer acquisition
- Customer retention
- Brand awareness
- Search visibility
