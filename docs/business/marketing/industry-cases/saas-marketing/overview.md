# SaaS Marketing Framework Overview

Specialized marketing guidance for Software-as-a-Service organizations with clear separation between MVP scope (2025) and Post-MVP roadmap (2026+) capabilities.

**MVP Focus:** Basic analytics, manual processes, 10% growth
**Post-MVP Focus:** Advanced analytics, optimization, 15% growth

## Document Structure

This framework is organized into focused modules:

1. **[Customer Lifecycle](/docs/business/marketing/industry-cases/saas-marketing/customer-lifecycle)** - Acquisition, retention, and expansion strategies
2. **[Campaigns](/docs/business/marketing/industry-cases/saas-marketing/campaigns)** - PLG and ABM campaign examples
3. **[Content and Technology](/docs/business/marketing/industry-cases/saas-marketing/content-technology)** - Content strategy and marketing technology stack
4. **[ROI Framework](/docs/business/marketing/industry-cases/saas-marketing/roi-framework)** - Metrics, benchmarks, and implementation

## MVP SaaS Marketing Environment

### MVP Industry Characteristics (2025)

- **Basic Revenue Model:** Subscription-based revenue with basic cash flow management
- **Customer Success Foundation:** Basic customer retention drives profitability
- **Product Foundation:** Basic product experience supports acquisition
- **Basic Analytics:** Basic analytics and user behavior tracking
- **Competitive Positioning:** Basic positioning and messaging for differentiation

### MVP Market Opportunities (2025)

- **Basic SaaS Market Growth:** Foundation for SaaS market participation
- **Vertical SaaS Foundation:** Basic industry-specific solution development
- **Basic Freemium Model:** Freemium model for basic customer acquisition
- **Basic Product Growth:** Foundation for product-led growth strategies

## Key Metrics Summary

| Metric | MVP Target | Post-MVP Target |
|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| MRR Growth | 5-10% MoM | 10-15% MoM |
| Trial-to-Paid | 12-18% | 18-22% |
| Monthly Churn | <8% | <6% |
| Net Revenue Retention | 105-115% | 115-125% |
| Expansion Revenue | 15-20% | 20-25% |
