# Anomaly Detection Framework

## Automated Anomaly Identification

### Health Score Anomaly Detection

```yaml
Health Anomaly Detection:
  Sudden Health Score Drops (>20 points):
    - Automated alert generation and stakeholder notification
    - Root cause analysis and contributing factor identification
    - Intervention planning and resource allocation
    - Recovery monitoring and success tracking
  
  Unusual Recovery Patterns:
    - Rapid improvement tracking and validation
    - Success factor identification and replication opportunities
    - Best practice development and knowledge sharing
    - Model optimization and prediction accuracy improvement
  
  Rapid Health Improvements:
    - Breakthrough pattern identification and analysis
    - Success factor correlation and validation
    - Intervention replication and scaling opportunities
    - Predictive model enhancement and optimization
  
  Consistent Health Plateaus:
    - Plateau pattern analysis and contributing factors
    - Engagement optimization and value demonstration strategies
    - Competitive positioning and differentiation reinforcement
    - Growth opportunity identification and pursuit
```

### Usage Pattern Anomaly Detection

```yaml
Usage Anomaly Framework:
  Dramatic Usage Decreases:
    - Early warning indicator generation and alert systems
    - Cause analysis and risk assessment protocols
    - Intervention planning and customer engagement strategies
    - Recovery tracking and success measurement
  
  Unexpected Usage Spikes:
    - Opportunity identification and expansion planning
    - Resource allocation and support optimization
    - Value demonstration and ROI communication
    - Growth strategy development and execution
  
  New Feature Adoption Patterns:
    - Adoption curve analysis and optimization opportunities
    - Training and support resource allocation
    - User feedback collection and product improvement
    - Success story development and best practice sharing
  
  User Behavior Shifts:
    - Behavioral pattern analysis and trend identification
    - Engagement strategy optimization and customization
    - Communication approach refinement and personalization
    - Relationship quality assessment and improvement planning
```

### Business Impact Anomaly Detection

```yaml
Business Impact Framework:
  Revenue Projection Variances:
    - Forecast accuracy monitoring and model optimization
    - Variance analysis and root cause investigation
    - Planning adjustment and resource reallocation
    - Stakeholder communication and expectation management
  
  Churn Rate Fluctuations:
    - Churn pattern analysis and trend identification
    - Risk factor assessment and mitigation strategy
    - Intervention effectiveness measurement and optimization
    - Competitive analysis and market positioning assessment
  
  Expansion Opportunity Changes:
    - Opportunity pipeline monitoring and qualification
    - Sales cycle optimization and bottleneck elimination
    - Value proposition refinement and communication
    - Success rate improvement and best practice development
  
  Stakeholder Engagement Shifts:
    - Engagement pattern analysis and relationship mapping
    - Communication optimization and personalization
    - Executive sponsor relationship management
    - Decision-maker influence mapping and engagement strategies
```

## Alert Configuration

| Anomaly Type | Threshold | Response Time | Escalation |
|--------------|-----------|---------------|------------|
| Health Drop >20 pts | Immediate | <5 minutes | CS Manager + Director |
| Usage Decrease >50% | 24 hours | <1 hour | CS Manager |
| Revenue Variance >10% | Weekly | <4 hours | CS Director + Finance |
| Churn Risk >80% | Daily | <30 minutes | CS Manager + Executive |
