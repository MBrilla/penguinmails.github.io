# Customer Health Optimization Overview

Step-by-step implementation guide for systematic customer health optimization, retention management, and expansion opportunity identification.

**Document Level:** Level 3 - Implementation Procedures
**Target Audience:** Customer Success Directors, Retention Managers, Customer Health Analysts
**Implementation Focus:** Practical procedures for health optimization and retention excellence

## Health Optimization Framework

### Monthly Health Monitoring Workflow

**Week 1: Data Collection & Analysis**

1. Extract monthly usage data from analytics platform
2. Calculate health scores using weighted component model
3. Identify health score changes >15 points from previous month
4. Flag accounts in At-Risk (<70) or Critical (<50) ranges
5. Generate monthly health report for CSM team review

**Week 2: Proactive Outreach Planning**

1. Prioritize accounts by health score decline severity and revenue impact
2. Assign CSM ownership for at-risk account interventions
3. Schedule proactive outreach calls with declining accounts
4. Prepare account-specific talking points and intervention strategies
5. Coordinate cross-functional support needs (technical, product, finance)

**Week 3: Intervention Execution**

1. Conduct scheduled health check calls with at-risk accounts
2. Document root causes of health decline in CRM system
3. Implement immediate interventions (technical fixes, training, support)
4. Establish success plans with clear metrics and timelines
5. Schedule follow-up touchpoints for progress monitoring

**Week 4: Results Tracking & Adjustment**

1. Monitor intervention effectiveness through usage trend analysis
2. Adjust intervention strategies based on early results
3. Document successful approaches for playbook refinement
4. Escalate critical accounts requiring executive engagement
5. Prepare monthly health optimization summary for leadership

## Health Monitoring Procedures

### Daily Health Monitoring (Automated Alerts)

**Critical Alert Triggers**:

- Account inactive for 14+ consecutive days → Immediate CSM notification
- Health score drops below 50 → Escalate to CSM director within 24 hours
- Payment failure or billing dispute → Alert CSM and finance team
- Support ticket marked "urgent" or "critical" → CSM review within 4 hours

**Alert Response Protocol**:

1. **Immediate Acknowledgment** (Within 2 hours): CSM reviews alert and account context
2. **Initial Assessment** (Within 4 hours): Determine if immediate intervention needed
3. **Action Plan** (Within 24 hours): Document intervention strategy in CRM
4. **Execution** (Within 48 hours): Initiate customer contact and intervention
5. **Follow-up** (72 hours): Confirm issue resolution and monitor health recovery

### Weekly Health Review (Team Coordination)

**CSM Team Stand-up Agenda** (30 minutes):

1. Review new at-risk accounts identified in past week
2. Share intervention successes and challenges
3. Coordinate cross-functional support needs
4. Identify expansion opportunities from healthy accounts
5. Update health optimization playbook with new learnings

**Account Portfolio Health Dashboard**:

- Total accounts by health score range (Healthy/Stable/At-Risk)
- Week-over-week health score trend analysis
- Intervention success rate tracking
- Expansion opportunity pipeline status
- Churn risk forecast for upcoming quarter
