# Channel Performance and ROI Analysis

## B2B Channel Cost Comparison

### Lead Generation Channel Economics

| Channel | Typical CPL | Typical CAC | Cost Driver | Best Use Case |
|---------|-------------|-------------|-------------|---------------|
| **B2B Cold Email** | $150-$225 | $510-$750 | Low tool/list cost | Volume prospecting |
| **Paid Search** | $70-$150 | $802-$1,200 | High CPC | Intent capture |
| **LinkedIn Ads** | $140-$300 | $982-$1,500 | High CPM | ABM, awareness |
| **SDR Phone** | $250-$840 | $1,500-$4,000+ | High personnel | Enterprise sales |
| **Content Marketing** | $50-$120 | $300-$600 | Content creation | Long-term value |
| **Webinars/Events** | $200-$500 | $800-$2,000 | Event costs | Thought leadership |

### Channel Performance by Funnel Stage

| Stage | Best Channel | Expected Metrics | Investment |
|-------|-------------|------------------|------------|
| **Awareness** | Content, Social | 2-5% CTR, 500-2K leads/mo | $5-15K/mo |
| **Consideration** | Email, LinkedIn | 1-3% CTR, 200-800 leads/mo | $3-10K/mo |
| **Evaluation** | Cold Email, SDR | 5-15% reply, 50-200 meetings/mo | $8-20K/mo |
| **Decision** | SDR Phone, Webinars | 20-40% meeting-to-deal | $10-25K/mo |

## Email Send Frequency Impact

| Frequency | Open Rate | Reply Rate | Unsubscribe | Effectiveness |
|-----------|-----------|------------|-------------|---------------|
| **Daily** | 15-20% | 2-3% | 2-5% | Low (spam-like) |
| **Every 2-3 days** | 25-30% | 4-6% | 0.5-1% | Good |
| **Weekly** | 30-35% | 6-8% | 0.1-0.3% | Excellent |
| **Bi-weekly** | 35-40% | 8-10% | 0.05-0.1% | Optimal |
| **Monthly** | 25-30% | 5-7% | 0.1-0.2% | Good |

## Optimal Send Patterns

### Cold Outreach Sequences

**Recommended Pattern**:
- **Days 1-7**: Daily sends (7 touchpoints)
- **Days 8-21**: Every 2-3 days (5-6 touchpoints)
- **Days 22-35**: Weekly sends (2 touchpoints)
- **Days 36+**: Monthly maintenance

**Expected Performance**:
- Total: 14-15 touchpoints over 35 days
- Reply rate: 8-12% combined
- Meeting rate: 2-4% combined

### Nurture Campaigns

**Recommended Pattern**:
- **Week 1-2**: Every 2-3 days (4-5 touchpoints)
- **Week 3-4**: Weekly sends (4 touchpoints)
- **Week 5+**: Bi-weekly ongoing

**Expected Performance**:
- Open rates: 40-60%
- Reply rates: 10-20%
