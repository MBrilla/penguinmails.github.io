# Go-to-Market Strategy

Phased go-to-market approach with success metrics and strategic documentation alignment.

## Phase 1: Beachhead Market (Months 1-6)

**Primary Target:** Mid-market agencies in North America

### Targets

- **Geographic Focus:** United States and Canada
- **Customer Count:** 50 customers
- **Revenue Target:** $50K MRR (aligned with $600K ARR target)
- **Customer Profile:** 10-50 employee agencies with 5-25 active clients

### Go-to-Market Channels

- **Content Marketing:** Cold email best practices, compliance guides
- **LinkedIn Outreach:** Direct sales to agency decision makers
- **Industry Partnerships:** Marketing technology partnerships
- **Referral Program:** Agency success story amplification

### Success Metrics

- Customer Acquisition: 8-10 customers per month
- Revenue Growth: $5-8K MRR growth per month
- Product-Market Fit: 85%+ customer satisfaction, <5% churn
- Market Validation: Positive unit economics, clear upgrade paths

## Phase 2: Market Expansion (Months 7-18)

**Primary Target:** Enterprise agencies and SaaS companies

### Targets

- **Geographic Expansion:** European markets (UK, Germany, Netherlands)
- **Customer Count:** 500 customers
- **Revenue Target:** $200K MRR (scaling from $50K to $200K MRR)
- **Customer Profile:** Enterprise agencies, mid-market SaaS companies

### Go-to-Market Channels

- **Paid Advertising:** LinkedIn Ads, Google Ads targeting key terms
- **Sales Team:** Dedicated enterprise sales team
- **Channel Partners:** Technology and service partner programs
- **Trade Shows:** Industry event participation and speaking

### Success Metrics

- Customer Acquisition: 30-50 customers per month
- Revenue Growth: $25-40K MRR growth per month
- Market Expansion: 20% international revenue
- Enterprise Adoption: 25% of revenue from enterprise customers

## Phase 3: Market Leadership (Month 19+)

**Primary Target:** Market dominance and adjacent opportunities

### Targets

- **Geographic Expansion:** Global expansion to APAC and LATAM
- **Customer Count:** 1,000+ customers
- **Revenue Target:** $1M ARR (market leadership position)
- **Market Position:** Top 3 cold email infrastructure platform

### Go-to-Market Channels

- **Enterprise Sales:** Large account sales team
- **Strategic Partnerships:** Technology acquisitions and partnerships
- **International Expansion:** Localized go-to-market strategies
- **Ecosystem Development:** Platform marketplace and developer community

### Success Metrics

- Market Share: 15-20% of cold email infrastructure market
- Customer Acquisition: 100+ customers per month
- Revenue Growth: $100K+ MRR growth per month
- Global Presence: 40% international revenue

## Strategic Documentation Alignment

### Primary Focus: Enterprise Agencies

- **Documentation Priority:** White-label capabilities, multi-tenant management
- **Content Strategy:** ROI calculators, compliance automation guides
- **Success Stories:** Agency case studies with revenue impact metrics

### Secondary Focus: Mid-Market Companies

- **Documentation Priority:** Setup automation, deliverability optimization
- **Content Strategy:** Technical implementation guides, best practices
- **Success Stories:** Scaling stories with technical depth

### Future Focus: High-Growth Startups

- **Documentation Priority:** Quick start guides, migration tools
- **Content Strategy:** Growth-focused content, viral features
- **Success Stories:** Rapid deployment and scaling examples

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|

| Net Revenue Retention | >110% |
| Customer Satisfaction (NPS) | >50% promoters |
| Feature Adoption | >70% using 3+ core features |

|--------|--------|

| Month-over-Month Revenue Growth | 20-30% |

|--------|--------|
