# E-commerce Metrics & Integration

Product development metrics, technology integration requirements, and success story frameworks.

## Product Development Metrics

### Customer Acquisition Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) | Cost per acquired customer across all channels |
| Conversion Rates | Lead-to-customer conversion and funnel optimization |
| Time to First Purchase | Customer time to first purchase and activation |
| Customer Quality | Customer quality metrics and long-term value potential |

### Customer Experience Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Net Promoter Score (NPS) | Customer satisfaction and advocacy measurement |
| Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) | Satisfaction with products and services |
| Customer Effort Score (CES) | Customer effort required for key interactions |
| Website Performance | Performance metrics and user experience scores |

### Customer Retention Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Customer Retention Rate | Customer retention and loyalty measurement |
| Churn Rate | Customer churn and loss prevention tracking |
| Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) | Long-term customer value optimization |
| Repeat Purchase Rate | Repeat purchase behavior and frequency |

### Business Impact Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Revenue Growth | Customer-driven revenue growth |
| Profitability | Customer profitability and margin optimization |
| Market Share | Customer acquisition impact on market share |
| Brand Value | Customer advocacy impact on brand value |
