# Operations-Customer Success Process Workflows

Integration workflows for customer health monitoring, intervention coordination, retention management, and expansion pipeline optimization.

## Customer Health Workflow

### Health Monitoring Process

1. **Data Collection**: Operations aggregates customer activity data from multiple sources
2. **Health Scoring**: Operations calculates health scores using predictive models
3. **Trend Analysis**: Operations analyzes health trajectories and patterns
4. **Alert Generation**: Operations triggers alerts for health score changes
5. **Intervention Routing**: Operations routes at-risk customers to appropriate teams
6. **Outcome Tracking**: Operations tracks intervention outcomes and success rates

### Health Score Components

| Component | Weight | Data Source |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Product Usage** | 35% | Application analytics |
| **Engagement** | 25% | Communication tracking |
| **Support Tickets** | 20% | Support system |
| **Payment History** | 10% | Billing system |
| **Feature Adoption** | 10% | Product analytics |

## Retention Operations Workflow

### Churn Prevention Process

1. **Risk Identification**: Operations identifies at-risk customers through predictive models
2. **Intervention Planning**: Operations coordinates intervention strategies with CS teams
3. **Outreach Coordination**: Operations triggers automated and manual outreach
4. **Response Tracking**: Operations monitors customer responses and engagement
5. **Escalation Management**: Operations manages escalation paths for critical cases
6. **Recovery Validation**: Operations validates recovery success and health improvement

### Intervention Types

| Risk Level | Intervention | Response Time |
|------------|--------------|---------------|
| **Critical** | Executive outreach, custom recovery plan | Same day |
| **High** | CSM escalation, value reinforcement | 24 hours |
| **Medium** | Automated nurture, feature training | 48 hours |
| **Low** | Proactive check-in, best practice sharing | Weekly |

## Expansion Pipeline Workflow

### Opportunity Identification

1. **Usage Analysis**: Operations identifies expansion signals from usage patterns
2. **Opportunity Scoring**: Operations scores opportunities based on success indicators
3. **Pipeline Creation**: Operations creates expansion opportunities in CRM
4. **Routing**: Operations routes opportunities to appropriate account teams
5. **Progression Tracking**: Operations monitors pipeline progression
6. **Success Attribution**: Operations tracks expansion outcomes and revenue

### Expansion Signals

| Signal Type | Indicator | Action |
|-------------|-----------|--------|
| **Usage Growth** | >150% of contracted capacity | Upgrade opportunity |
| **Feature Interest** | Premium feature exploration | Feature upsell |
| **Team Growth** | New user invitations | Seat expansion |
| **Use Case Expansion** | New workflow adoption | Module upsell |
