# Operations-Marketing Process Workflows

Integration workflows for campaign coordination, content production, distribution optimization, and performance measurement.

## Campaign Operations Workflow

### Campaign Planning and Execution

1. **Resource Planning**: Operations coordinates campaign resource allocation with marketing timelines
2. **Content Coordination**: Operations integrates content production with campaign schedules
3. **Distribution Planning**: Operations optimizes distribution timing across channels
4. **Performance Setup**: Operations establishes performance monitoring and triggers
5. **Quality Validation**: Operations validates brand consistency and compliance
6. **Success Measurement**: Operations tracks campaign success against objectives

### Content Production Workflow

1. **Production Planning**: Operations coordinates content creation with marketing needs
2. **Workflow Automation**: Operations implements automated approval processes
3. **Quality Assurance**: Operations validates content against brand standards
4. **Distribution Coordination**: Operations optimizes content distribution timing
5. **Performance Tracking**: Operations monitors content engagement and impact
6. **Optimization Triggers**: Operations identifies content optimization opportunities

## Performance Tracking Framework

### Real-Time Monitoring

| Metric Category | Monitoring Frequency | Action Threshold |
|-----------------|---------------------|------------------|
| **Campaign Performance** | Real-time | Below target triggers optimization |
| **Content Engagement** | Hourly | Low engagement triggers adjustment |
| **Distribution Success** | Real-time | Delivery failures trigger response |
| **Resource Utilization** | Daily | Overallocation triggers rebalancing |

### Optimization Triggers

- **Performance Below Target**: Automated adjustment of campaign parameters
- **Engagement Drop**: Content optimization or channel adjustment
- **Delivery Issues**: Alternative channel activation or timing change
- **Resource Constraints**: Dynamic reallocation and priority adjustment

## Cross-Domain Coordination

### Marketing-Operations Handoffs

| Stage | Marketing Responsibility | Operations Responsibility |
|-------|-------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Planning** | Campaign objectives | Resource allocation |
| **Production** | Content creation | Workflow coordination |
| **Distribution** | Channel strategy | Timing optimization |
| **Monitoring** | Performance analysis | System monitoring |
| **Optimization** | Strategy adjustment | Process improvement |
