# Operations-Sales Integration Overview

This document establishes comprehensive operations-sales integration requirements, defining data flows, process coordination, workflow synchronization, and cross-domain optimization between operations and sales domains.

Operations-sales integration enables seamless coordination between operational processes and sales activities, driving lead management efficiency, pipeline optimization, customer acquisition excellence, and revenue operations alignment.
