# Operations-Sales Process Workflows

Integration workflows for lead management, pipeline optimization, customer onboarding handoff, and sales performance coordination.

## Lead Processing Workflow

### Lead Management Process

1. **Lead Capture**: Operations captures leads from all sources
2. **Data Verification**: Operations verifies lead data accuracy and completeness
3. **Enrichment**: Operations enriches lead data with additional information
4. **Qualification Scoring**: Operations scores leads for qualification
5. **Routing**: Operations routes qualified leads to sales teams
6. **Conversion Tracking**: Operations tracks lead-to-opportunity conversion

### Lead Quality Framework

| Stage | Operations Role | Outcome |
|-------|-----------------|---------|
| **Capture** | Source tracking, data collection | Complete lead records |
| **Verification** | Data validation, duplicate detection | Clean lead data |
| **Enrichment** | Company/contact data addition | Rich lead profiles |
| **Scoring** | Qualification scoring | Prioritized lead queue |
| **Routing** | Team/rep assignment | Optimal lead distribution |

## Pipeline Management Workflow

### Pipeline Operations Process

1. **Pipeline Monitoring**: Operations monitors pipeline health and progression
2. **Stage Analysis**: Operations analyzes stage conversion rates and bottlenecks
3. **Hygiene Maintenance**: Operations maintains pipeline data quality
4. **Forecasting Support**: Operations provides pipeline-based forecasts
5. **Optimization Triggers**: Operations triggers optimization actions
6. **Performance Reporting**: Operations reports pipeline performance

### Stage Optimization

| Stage | Conversion Target | Operations Focus |
|-------|------------------|------------------|
| **Lead** | 30% to opportunity | Qualification optimization |
| **Discovery** | 50% to demo | Engagement tracking |
| **Demo** | 60% to proposal | Demo effectiveness |
| **Proposal** | 40% to negotiation | Proposal optimization |
| **Negotiation** | 70% to close | Deal velocity |

## Customer Onboarding Handoff

### Sales-to-CS Handoff Process

1. **Deal Closure**: Sales completes deal and gathers customer information
2. **Handoff Preparation**: Operations prepares customer profile for CS
3. **Success Planning**: Operations initiates success plan template
4. **Team Assignment**: Operations assigns CS team members
5. **Kickoff Coordination**: Operations schedules customer kickoff
6. **Progress Tracking**: Operations tracks onboarding progress

### Handoff Checklist

| Item | Owner | Timing |
|------|-------|--------|
| **Customer profile** | Sales | At close |
| **Contract details** | Operations | Day 0 |
| **Success plan** | CS | Day 1 |
| **Kickoff meeting** | All | Day 3-5 |
| **Milestone tracking** | Operations | Ongoing |
