# Operations-Finance Process Workflows

Integration workflows for budget management, cost optimization, revenue recognition, and financial reporting coordination.

## Budget Management Workflow

### Budget Planning and Tracking

1. **Budget Planning**: Operations coordinates budget planning with financial targets
2. **Allocation Optimization**: Operations optimizes budget allocation across departments
3. **Expense Tracking**: Operations monitors expenses against budget allocations
4. **Variance Analysis**: Operations analyzes variances and identifies optimization opportunities
5. **Reallocation Triggers**: Operations triggers budget reallocations based on performance
6. **Performance Reporting**: Operations generates budget performance reports

### Budget Optimization Framework

| Process | Frequency | Outcome |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Planning** | Annual/Quarterly | Budget targets aligned with strategy |
| **Allocation** | Monthly | Optimized resource distribution |
| **Tracking** | Weekly | Expense monitoring and control |
| **Variance Analysis** | Weekly | Performance gap identification |
| **Reallocation** | Monthly | Dynamic budget optimization |

## Cost Optimization Workflow

### Cost Management Process

1. **Cost Analysis**: Operations analyzes costs across all operational areas
2. **Opportunity Identification**: Operations identifies cost reduction opportunities
3. **Strategy Development**: Operations develops optimization strategies
4. **Implementation Coordination**: Operations coordinates implementation with teams
5. **Efficiency Tracking**: Operations monitors efficiency improvements
6. **Savings Validation**: Operations validates realized cost savings

### Cost Categories

| Category | Analysis Focus | Optimization Target |
|----------|---------------|---------------------|
| **Infrastructure** | Cloud, servers, software | 20% reduction |
| **Personnel** | Productivity, utilization | 15% efficiency gain |
| **Operations** | Process efficiency | 25% cost reduction |
| **Vendor** | Contract optimization | 10% savings |

## Revenue Recognition Workflow

### Revenue Operations Process

1. **Revenue Tracking**: Operations tracks revenue from all sources
2. **Attribution Analysis**: Operations attributes revenue to sources and campaigns
3. **Recognition Processing**: Operations processes revenue recognition per standards
4. **Performance Analysis**: Operations analyzes revenue performance trends
5. **Forecasting**: Operations provides revenue forecasts based on trends
6. **Reporting**: Operations generates revenue reports for stakeholders

### Financial Reporting Workflow

1. **Data Consolidation**: Operations consolidates financial data from all systems
2. **Report Generation**: Operations generates standard and custom reports
3. **Analysis Preparation**: Operations prepares analysis and insights
4. **Distribution**: Operations distributes reports to stakeholders
5. **Review Coordination**: Operations coordinates report reviews
6. **Action Tracking**: Operations tracks actions from report findings
