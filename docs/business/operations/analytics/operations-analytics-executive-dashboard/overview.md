## Analytics Governance Framework

### Governance Structure

- **Executive Ownership**: C-level defines analytics priorities, security metrics, and compliance KPIs
- **Operations Director Accountability**: Operations analytics accuracy and cross-domain validation
- **Security Analytics Standards**: Security team manages threat analytics and posture validation
- **Compliance Analytics Standards**: Compliance team manages regulatory analytics and audit validation
- **Cross-Domain Alignment**: Domain leads ensure data flow and metric consistency

---

## Executive Performance Dashboard

Real-time strategic performance monitoring with actionable insights.

### Performance Dashboard Components

- **Operations Health Score**: Real-time composite operations health assessment
- **KPI Trend Analysis**: 30/90-day KPI trajectories and performance predictions
- **Alert System**: Automated alerts for KPI threshold breaches and optimization opportunities
- **Comparative Analysis**: Performance comparison against targets and industry benchmarks

### Executive Dashboard Views

- **Strategic Overview**: High-level KPI summary with business impact assessment
- **Operational Detail**: Detailed operational metrics with optimization recommendations
- **Predictive Analytics**: Forward-looking KPI predictions and scenario planning
- **Cross-Domain Impact**: KPI correlation with other business domain performance
