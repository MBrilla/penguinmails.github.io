# Marketing Agency Use Cases

Use cases for Marketing Agency owners managing multiple client email campaigns.

## Use Case 1: Multi-Client Campaign Management

### Primary Actor

Marketing Agency Owner

### Goal

Manage multiple client email campaigns efficiently with professional results

### Preconditions

- Agency has multiple client accounts set up
- Client-specific branding and templates are configured
- Client email lists and customer data are available
- Campaign objectives and success metrics are defined

### Main Success Scenario

**1. Client Onboarding and Setup**
- New client signs contract and agency sets up client account
- Agency configures client-specific branding and templates
- Client email lists are imported and validated
- Initial campaign strategy and objectives are established
- Client success metrics and reporting requirements are defined

**2. Campaign Development and Execution**
- Agency develops client-specific email campaigns and content
- Campaigns are scheduled and deployed to client email lists
- Performance monitoring and optimization throughout campaign execution
- Regular client reporting and communication
- Campaign results are analyzed and strategies are optimized

**3. Client Success and Retention**
- Client satisfaction is measured and maintained at high levels
- Campaign performance meets or exceeds client objectives
- Agency demonstrates clear ROI and business value
- Client renewals and expansions are secured
- Agency grows client base through successful outcomes

### Alternative Scenarios

| Scenario | Response |
|----------|----------|
| Low Performance | Campaign underperforms requiring strategy pivot |
| Client Changes | Client requirements change mid-campaign |
| Deliverability Issues | Email deliverability problems require immediate attention |
| Competitive Pressure | Market changes require competitive response strategies |

### Success Criteria

- All client campaigns are executed successfully and on time
- Client satisfaction scores remain above 90%
- Agency maintains high client retention and expansion rates
- Revenue growth through successful client outcomes

## Use Case 2: Agency Scaling and Growth

### Primary Actor

Marketing Agency Owner

### Goal

Scale agency operations and grow revenue while maintaining quality

### Preconditions

- Agency has proven client success and case studies
- Standardized processes and templates are established
- Team capacity planning and hiring strategy is in place
- Growth funding and investment planning is complete

### Main Success Scenario

**1. Operational Scaling**
- Agency implements standardized processes and automation
- Team members are hired and trained on agency workflows
- Client onboarding and management systems are optimized
- Quality assurance and review processes are established
- Technology and tools are upgraded to support increased volume

**2. Market Expansion**
- Agency pursues new client acquisition through referrals and marketing
- Service offerings are expanded to capture more revenue per client
- Agency develops industry specialization and expertise
- Strategic partnerships and referral networks are established
- Market positioning and competitive differentiation is strengthened

**3. Revenue Optimization**
- Pricing strategies are optimized for profitability and competitiveness
- High-value client services and premium offerings are developed
- Client retention and expansion strategies are implemented
- Agency achieves sustainable growth and profitability targets
- Exit strategies or acquisition opportunities are explored

### Alternative Scenarios

| Scenario | Response |
|----------|----------|
| Quality Challenges | Rapid scaling threatens service quality |
| Talent Shortage | Difficulty hiring qualified team members |
| Market Saturation | Competitive pressure limits growth |
| Operational Complexity | Scaling creates process challenges |

### Success Criteria

- Agency scales operations without compromising service quality
- Revenue growth targets are achieved consistently
- Client retention rates remain high during scaling
- Agency establishes market leadership position
