# Enterprise Team Use Cases

Use cases for Enterprise Marketing Operations and IT teams managing email marketing at scale.

## Use Case 7: Enterprise Campaign Management

### Primary Actor

Enterprise Marketing Operations Manager

### Goal

Coordinate enterprise-wide email marketing with brand consistency and governance

### Preconditions

- Enterprise hierarchy and departmental structure is defined
- Brand guidelines and governance policies are established
- Approval workflows and compliance requirements are documented
- Integration with enterprise systems is configured

### Main Success Scenario

**1. Enterprise Governance Setup**
- Enterprise email marketing policies and guidelines are established
- Departmental permissions and access controls are configured
- Brand templates and approval workflows are implemented
- Compliance and regulatory requirements are integrated
- Performance monitoring and reporting systems are established

**2. Coordinated Campaign Execution**
- Cross-departmental email campaigns are planned and coordinated
- Brand consistency is maintained across all departments
- Approval processes are followed for all campaigns
- Performance metrics are tracked and reported at enterprise level
- Lessons learned and best practices are shared across departments

**3. Enterprise Performance Optimization**
- Enterprise-wide performance analysis and optimization
- Cross-departmental collaboration and knowledge sharing
- Technology and platform optimization for enterprise scale
- Strategic planning and goal alignment across organization
- ROI measurement and business impact reporting

### Alternative Scenarios

| Scenario | Response |
|----------|----------|
| Governance Challenges | Inconsistent compliance and brand adherence |
| Departmental Conflicts | Competing priorities and resource allocation |
| Technology Limitations | Platform scalability and integration challenges |
| Compliance Violations | Regulatory or policy compliance issues |

### Success Criteria

- Consistent brand messaging and governance across enterprise
- Improved operational efficiency and campaign effectiveness
- Strong cross-departmental collaboration and coordination
- Measurable enterprise-wide business impact and ROI

## Use Case 8: Enterprise Integration and Data Management

### Primary Actor

Enterprise IT and Marketing Operations

### Goal

Integrate email marketing with enterprise systems and maintain data governance

### Preconditions

- Enterprise system architecture and integration requirements
- Data governance and privacy policies are defined
- IT security and compliance requirements are established
- Integration development resources and timeline are available

### Main Success Scenario

**1. System Integration Implementation**
- Email marketing platform is integrated with enterprise CRM
- Customer data synchronization and workflow automation
- API development and system connectivity
- Data validation and quality assurance processes
- Performance monitoring and reliability management

**2. Data Governance and Compliance**
- Enterprise data governance policies are implemented
- Customer data privacy and protection compliance
- Audit trails and data lineage tracking
- Data retention and deletion procedures
- Security controls and access management

**3. Performance and Optimization**
- System performance monitoring and optimization
- Data quality and consistency maintenance
- Integration reliability and error handling
- Scalability planning and capacity management
- Continuous improvement and optimization processes

### Alternative Scenarios

| Scenario | Response |
|----------|----------|
| Data Quality Issues | Integration errors and data inconsistencies |
| Security Concerns | Data breaches or compliance violations |
| Performance Problems | System slowdowns or reliability issues |
| Integration Failures | Connectivity or data synchronization problems |

### Success Criteria

- Reliable, secure integration with enterprise systems
- High data quality and consistency across platforms
- Strong compliance and governance framework
- Scalable, performant system architecture

## Enterprise Integration Requirements

### Technical Requirements

| Category | Requirements |
|----------|--------------|
| CRM Integration | Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics |
| Data Sync | Real-time or near-real-time synchronization |
| API Access | RESTful APIs with OAuth 2.0 authentication |
| SSO | SAML 2.0 or OAuth-based single sign-on |
| Audit Logging | Complete audit trail for compliance |

### Governance Requirements

| Category | Requirements |
|----------|--------------|
| Access Control | Role-based access with departmental isolation |
| Approval Workflows | Multi-level approval for campaign launch |
| Brand Templates | Locked templates with limited customization |
| Compliance Checks | Automated compliance validation |
| Reporting | Enterprise-wide analytics and dashboards |
